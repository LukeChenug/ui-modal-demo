Call for Papers

At Compile, we want to hear from you! We're taking open submissions for our Chalkboard Stage.

If chosen, you'll step up with nothing but a board and a piece of chalk to work through your idea live, from the ground up.

Example types of topics:
  • Rethinking a core system from scratch
  • A hard-won lesson from something you built
  • New mental models for AI-native development
  • Explaining a complex concept simply

For further inspiration, check out Kurt Vonnegut's iconic chalkboard lecture on the Shapes of Stories.

FAQs

Can I bring my team? Can I transfer my invite?+
Compile is invite-only, but if you want to recommend another attendee or send someone else from your team, reply to your invitation or email compile@cursor.com.
Who is Compile designed for?+
Engineers, researchers, and builders who want to think seriously about software: how it is made, how it changes, and what we can build next.
Will the event be accessible to my needs?+
The event will be ADA accessible, with food options to accommodate all allergens. Please share any specific needs with our team in your registration.
